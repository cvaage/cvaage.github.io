As a life-long student, I seek out means by which I can simultaneously learn about and build my community - a motivation that consistently leads me to the intersection of research and education.

### Future Rivers, UW Earth Lab
Following the week-long Summer Institute, my cohort and I created an educational display to be housed in the Fisheries Science building at the University of Washington.
[Check out our exhibit here](https://futurerivers.uw.edu/2024/05/28/elwha-summer-institute-product/)
