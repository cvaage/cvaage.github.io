About a decade ago, I began curating my education and experiences to build a comprehensive understanding of how we interact with our world. This ambition led me through a diverse spread of ecosystems, agencies, and communities, contributing to my identity as an environmental steward. I intend for this website to be a platform to share my research interests, past engagements, and current work with potential collaborators, advisers, and friends ( and reflect with myself ! ).
